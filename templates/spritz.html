About Me

Bookmarklet is here: Readsy. Drag it onto your bookmarks bar and click it from the webpage of a particularly tedious article!

Readsy is a tool to help you skim large amounts of text by focusing your eyes on one word at a time without having to move them. It is powered by Spritz - you can read more about it here.


Akash Jain is a sophomore at Princeton University majoring in Computer Science.

If this site helped you in a meaningful way, please consider donating a little bit to help me with running costs :)

This is a little experiment to make it easier to read longform content. The backend is made using Flask, and uses APIs and modules from Readability, BeautifulSoup, PDFMiner, Requests, and more. The front end uses Bootstrap 3, HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery and Froala.
